Who are we and what is our goal

We all started out the same... Going on holiday to destinations which we had already been to before, in over populated areas of the world, over priced and over hyped. We all have one thing in common, and that is we strive for adventure. We all met in unique and unexpected corners of the world and now share a passion to give our experience to as many people as possible.

It is our goal to give you a unique holiday experience, which you will remember for years to come, and which will open your eyes to the real world.

“THE WORLD IS A BOOK AND THOSE WHO DO NOT TRAVEL READ ONLY A PAGE.”
~ SAINT AUGUSTINE

What gave us our name

After we got together, we would often share our holiday experiences with other friends and family and found that we were getting more and more questions on where we would recommend for a holiday destination.

Before long we decided that we would make it our full time work to give people the best holiday experiences that we could, we found that understanding the person and what they look for in life has helped us to provide the best experience possible.
